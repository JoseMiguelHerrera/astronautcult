About Astronaut Cult

Astronaut Cult is an electronic dance music and art project. It has the goal of moving both the soul and the body using deep basslines, hypnotic rhythms, and touching melodies. Deeply rooted in the traditions of house, hiphop, and jungle, but always looking to the future. Leave the city and come with me on the trip of a lifetime.
